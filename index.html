Arknights Favorite Picker

made by alyssushi#2239

Includes all operators and main story NPCs, but side story NPCs have yet to be added. Stay tuned...

This tool will present you with a few characters at a time for you to choose your favorite from. (You can also choose multiple at a time.) Once you've made your selection, press Pick to move to the next round. If you aren't sure, you can also pass the round. However, passing doesn't provide any new information, so passing repeatedly won't get you anywhere.

You can keep picking after a favorite is found to build a list of favorites as long as you like. Since the process works via elimination, characters should show up under Favorite Characters in descending order, starting with your top favorite character. (This also means that if you keep picking until no characters are left, you'll end up with a ranking list similar to a character sorter.)

If needed, you can also re-order your favorites by dragging them around. Refresh the page to reset your progress.

I'm not smart enough to code all this myself. This was made using this template from The Cave of Dragonflies. Character icons are taken from Aceship's Toolbox and the Fandom Wiki.
